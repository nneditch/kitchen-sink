The Blue Issue

Although editors offered no overt theme for this issue, KS8 has come together as a cohesive whole. In retrospect, we can say with confidence that we wanted to rely on the natural currents that develop within a magazine on which so many people collaborate so closely. In reality, we felt set adrift at the prospect of assigning stories for a themeless issue. KS7, the hook-up issue, helped us realize that working together with an awareness of the way the magazine is shaping up provides plenty of byways for correspondence throughout the issue, which allows readers to discover for themselves the ways text and illustration reach for each other and mingle, nuancing and amplifying meaning.
